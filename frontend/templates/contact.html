About the Project

Research PDF File Renamer is a free, web-based tool designed to help researchers and students organize their PDF documents by automatically generating descriptive filenames based on the content of academic papers. The service uses artificial intelligence to extract metadata such as author names, publication year, journal titles, and keywords from PDF documents.
