This Privacy Policy describes how Research PDF File Renamer ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our web-based tool. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name provided during registration

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the PDF renaming service

4. PDF Document Processing

How Your PDFs Are Handled

  • Uploaded PDFs are processed temporarily to extract metadata (author, title, year, journal)

The Service may use third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI or similar services) to analyze PDF content. When a PDF is processed, extracted text may be sent to these providers. Please review the privacy policies of any third-party AI services configured by the administrator.

Account information is retained for as long as your account is active. Uploaded PDF files are deleted automatically after processing. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting the administrator.
